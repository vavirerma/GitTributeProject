Tribute to Virat Kohli

A Legend of Modern Cricket

Virat Kohli is widely regarded as one of the best cricketers of his generation. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, and his journey to becoming one of the all-time greats in cricket has been nothing short of extraordinary.

Career Highlights

  • Leading run scorer in international cricket among active players
  • ICC Men's Cricketer of the Decade (2011-2020)
  • Fastest to 8000, 9000, 10000, 11000, and 12000 ODI runs
  • Captained India to numerous memorable wins across formats
  • Consistent performer across Test, ODI, and T20 formats
